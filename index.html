Our Story

Born During a Storm,
Built with Heart

The Endless Kettle was founded in 2020 by Michael, right in the midst of the Covid pandemic. What started as a bold leap of faith has grown into a beloved neighbourhood gathering place in a beautifully restored Victorian bank on North Street, Keighley.

Our kitchen blends classic English breakfasts and lunches with a modern twist and a touch of Polish influence. Every guest is treated like family.

What We Serve

Menu Highlights

Classic English fare with Polish soul and Korean fire. Here are a few of our favourites.

The Rustic Breakfast

Fan Favourite

Our signature full English with a rustic twist. Locally sourced sausages, crispy bacon, free-range eggs, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, beans, toast & black pudding.

Croque Madame

Chef's Pick

A magnificent stack of toasted brioche, ham, melted gruyere, bechamel sauce, topped with a perfectly fried free-range egg.

Sweet or Savoury Dutch Baby

A golden, oven-puffed pancake served your way — go sweet with fresh berries and cream, or savoury with bacon, cheese, and herbs.

Steak, Eggs & Crispy Sumac Potatoes

Perfectly seared steak with free-range eggs and golden crispy potatoes dusted with fragrant sumac. A hearty favourite.

Short Rib Lasagne

Signature

Slow-cooked short rib lasagne, fried in crispy panko breadcrumbs, served atop our house marinara and covered in freshly grated grana padano.

EK French Toast

Thick-cut brioche dipped in our secret cinnamon batter, golden-fried and served with fresh berries, maple syrup, and a dusting of icing sugar.

We cater to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies.
