Korean Fried Chicken

Bold yet Comforting. Flavour Rich. Delicious.

Our Signature

A Labour of Love

Bold yet Comforting. Flavour Rich. Delicious.

Created by founder Mike, our Korean Fried Chicken features succulent chicken pieces marinated in a special blend of spices, double-fried to achieve the perfect crunch, and coated in a rich, spicy glaze.

Every batch is made fresh in-house with careful preparation. The process takes time, but it delivers a crispy, flavourful delight that is absolutely worth the wait.

100% Gluten Free Double Fried Made with Love
The Process

Crafted to Perfection

From marination to the double-fry, every step is designed to deliver the ultimate crunch and flavour.

🧂 Marinated

Each piece is lovingly marinated in a special blend of spices, allowing the flavours to penetrate deep into the chicken for maximum taste.

🍳 Double Fried

Our double-fry technique is the secret to that legendary crunch. The first fry cooks the chicken through, the second creates the irresistible crispy exterior.

🌶 Glazed

Finished with a rich, spicy glaze that perfectly complements the crunch. Bold, savoury, with just the right amount of heat.
