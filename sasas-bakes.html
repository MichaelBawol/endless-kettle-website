Sasa's Bakes

Handcrafted with love each morning

In-House Bakery

Meet Saskia

Meet Saskia — our founder's mum and the heart of our in-house bakery. For Sasa, baking is not just a skill but an art form, and passion is her secret ingredient.

She blends traditional Polish recipes with modern twists, creating bakes that are both comforting and exciting. Every item is handcrafted with the finest ingredients each morning, ensuring nothing but the freshest treats make it to your table.

We also offer healthy options, including vegan and vegetarian choices, so everyone can enjoy a little something special.

What We Bake

Our Creations

From traditional Polish pastries to indulgent cheesecakes, every bake is a small masterpiece.

🍩 Polish Pastries & Paczki

Traditional

Traditional buttery buns and Polish donuts that bring the flavours of Poland to Keighley. From classic glazed varieties to adventurous seasonal fillings, each one is a taste of home.

🍰 Cakes & Sweet Treats

Fan Favourite

Fluffy cinnamon rolls, freshly baked muffins, and decadent cakes for any occasion. Whether it's a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon indulgence, there's always something tempting.

🧀 Cheesecakes

Our Pride & Joy

Our pride and joy. Choose from the classic Krakowian-style cheesecake or the indulgent Basque variety. Each one is made with the freshest ingredients and lots of love.

🎂 Custom Orders

Made for You

Birthdays, anniversaries, or any special event — Sasa will create something beautiful just for you. Every custom order is crafted with care and tailored to your wishes.
